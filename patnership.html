Partnership Conditions

Before applying to become a partner, please review our partnership conditions:

  1. Partners must align with Primetech Foundation's mission and values.
  2. Financial commitments must be fulfilled as agreed in the partnership terms.
  3. Partners are expected to maintain ethical business practices.
  4. Brand usage guidelines must be followed when referencing the partnership.
  5. Partnerships are subject to annual review for renewal.
  6. Both parties must agree to transparent communication and reporting.

By submitting the partnership form, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to these conditions.
