Our Story

Millions of youths in slum areas face unemployment
due to a lack of technical skills and opportunities.
PrimeTech Foundation aims to bridge this gap by
equiping them with digital skills, thereby increasing
their chances of securing remote jobs, freelancing
opportunities or starting their own tech-based ventures.

Our Mission

To empower youths in slum areas with digital skills, fostering self-employment and reducing unemployment.

Our Vision

A world where every youth has access to digital education and opportunities for economic growth.

Our Objectives

  1. Provide digital skills training to 500+ youths annually.
  2. Facilitate mentorship and career guidance.
  3. Partner with companies to offer internship and job placement opportunities.
  4. Support graduates in launching digital startups.
  5. Promote digital inclusion and technology-driven solutions in underprivileged communities.
